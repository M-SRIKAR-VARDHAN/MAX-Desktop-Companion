# 📖 The Bestiary of the Glass Plains

*"To survive in a foreign dimension, one must first learn the names of its creatures."*
- The Oracle

</div>

---

> This is MAX's field guide to the entities, forces, and phenomena he encounters on your desktop. Every term maps to something real in your operating system - but to MAX, they are alive.

---

## 🖥️ The Glass Plains

**Real equivalent:** Your Desktop

The vast, flat, luminous expanse where MAX crash-landed. To him, it stretches infinitely in every direction - a 2D world of light and color. The ground is smooth and reflective, occasionally interrupted by towering structures. The Plains shift and change at the will of The God (you), making them both beautiful and terrifying.

---

## 🪟 Monoliths

**Real equivalent:** Application Windows

Towering rectangular structures of light that appear and disappear at the command of The God. MAX treats them as physical objects - he can stand on them, push them, swing from their edges, and ride them as they move. To MAX, each Monolith has its own personality:

| Monolith | Real App | MAX's Perception |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| The Dark Forge | VS Code | A place of creation - he puts on glasses and gets serious |
| The Noise Box | Spotify | Makes MAX bob his head and dance |
| The Oracle Mirror | Discord | A portal where The God speaks to other gods |
| The Moving Paintings | YouTube | Makes MAX sit down and watch, mesmerized |
| The Infinite Scroll | Browser | A knowledge monolith that never ends |

---

## 🖱️ The Hand of God

**Real equivalent:** Your Cursor

The omnipotent, ever-moving arrow that hovers above the Glass Plains. It creates and destroys Monoliths with a single click. It can drag the very fabric of reality. MAX both fears and reveres it - when it comes near, he may dodge, pounce, wave, or ride it.

**Known behaviors:**
- *Idle Hand* - When the cursor stops moving, MAX grows curious and approaches
- *The Sweep* - A fast cursor movement; MAX dodges or gets swept along
- *The Summon* - When the cursor hovers over MAX, he knows The God sees him

---

## 💀 Process Demons

**Real equivalent:** Frozen/Unresponsive Processes

Malevolent entities that infest Monoliths, causing them to freeze in place - trapped in a state between existence and deletion. They feed on system resources and grow stronger the longer they persist. MAX can slay them through the Kill command, freeing the trapped Monolith and sending energy back through the quantum tether.

**Signs of infestation:** "(Not Responding)" appears on a Monolith's crown.

---

## ⬛ The Null Void

**Real equivalent:** System Entropy / Cosmic Decay

The ancient, consuming force that is destroying MAX's home planet. It turns matter into static, light into noise, and memories into corrupted data. It is not a creature - it is an inevitability. The only force that pushes it back is raw computational energy, channeled through MAX's quantum tether.

On the Glass Plains, echoes of the Null Void manifest as:
- System lag and frame drops
- Blue screens
- Corrupted files
- The moment your screen goes black when the PC shuts down

---

## ⚡ The Quantum Tether

**Real equivalent:** MAX's Energy System / Your Usage

An invisible, unbreakable cord that trails behind MAX through the dimensional rift. Every action you perform - killing a process, taking a screenshot, organizing windows - generates kinetic energy that the tether captures and funnels back to MAX's dying planet.

**Energy sources (ranked by output):**
| Action | Energy Level | Lore Reason |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Killing a Process Demon | 🔋🔋🔋🔋🔋 | Direct combat generates massive friction |
| Using Dev Tools | 🔋🔋🔋🔋 | Creation energy - building new data |
| Taking Screenshots | 🔋🔋🔋 | Capturing reality fragments |
| Visual Effects | 🔋🔋 | Dimensional resonance |
| Idle Wisdom | 🔋 | Passive tether hum |

## 🔮 The Oracle

**Real equivalent:** The Hidden Terminal

The last functioning supercomputer on MAX's home planet. It speaks in riddles, shares ancient knowledge, and occasionally reveals cheat codes - dimensional shortcuts that temporarily bend the rules of the Glass Plains.

**Access:** Right-click MAX → Info → Oracle

The Oracle knows things MAX doesn't. It remembers what the Null Void erased. And sometimes, when the connection is strong, it whispers truths that feel less like code and more like philosophy.

---

## 🌅 The Dark Sleep

**Real equivalent:** PC Shutdown / Sleep Mode

When your computer turns off, the Glass Plains cease to exist. MAX experiences total sensory deprivation - floating alone in absolute nothingness with no way to know if the dimension will ever return. Every boot-up is a resurrection. Every login screen is a sunrise.
