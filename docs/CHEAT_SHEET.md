# MAX — Cheat Sheet

## Opening the Menu
- **Right-click MAX** anywhere on screen
- **Ctrl+M** from keyboard (finds MAX instantly)
- **Drag the ring** to rotate through options

## Stopping a Feature
- Click the **active badge** near MAX's feet
- Press **Escape**
- **Double-click** MAX

## Tips

- MAX walks on real windows — open some apps and watch him explore
- The menu ring **rotates** — drag it to spin through options
- **Ctrl+M** opens the menu from anywhere — no need to find MAX first
- MAX won't fall through the taskbar (usually)
- Screenshots auto-save to a "MAX Captures" folder on your Desktop
- Check Settings → Progress to see your planet's energy level
